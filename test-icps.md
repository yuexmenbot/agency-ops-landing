- **Location**: Austin, TX

**Personality:**
- Creative, visual thinker
- Skeptical of "corporate" processes  
- Values team culture and creativity
- Quick decision maker when convinced
- Fears losing the "boutique" feel

**Response to Landing Page:**
- ✅ Resonates with "making less than solo" message
- ✅ Before/After numbers are compelling
- ⚠️ Might worry about "making us corporate"
- ✅ ROI calculator shows clear value
- ✅ Sebastian's peer credentials matter

## Testing Questions for Each ICP

### Messaging Effectiveness:
1. Does the headline grab attention immediately?
2. Is the pain point description accurate and compelling?
3. Does Sebastian's credibility feel peer-to-peer vs. consultant-to-client?
4. Is the ROI calculator useful and believable?

### Objection Handling:
1. Does the "corporate" concern get addressed effectively?
2. Are there other unstated objections not covered?
3. Is the scarcity ("1 agency this quarter") believable?
4. Does the investment range feel justified by the ROI?

### Call-to-Action:
1. Is the primary CTA (email Sebastian) clear and compelling?
2. Are there appropriate friction reducers (30-min discovery call)?
3. Should there be additional CTAs for different comfort levels?

### Design & UX:
1. Does the mobile experience work well?
2. Is the stone/amber palette professional but not corporate?
3. Does the page flow logically from problem to solution to action?
4. Are the key proof points prominently displayed?
